PAINT MIST & DUST CONTROL

Paint mist, dust and spray exhaust treatment engineering

For the painting, grinding, polishing, cutting and surface treatment processes, paint mist interception, dust collection, exhaust duct and fan systems are planned to reduce suspended particles and working environment pollution.

Engineering goals

Capture:Collect paint mist and dust close to the pollution source

filter:Water washing, dry filter material or filter cartridge dust collection

exhaust:Maintain negative pressure and stable ventilation

Safety:Reduce dust accumulation and fire risk

Source capture

Planning the spray booth, hoods and exhaust outlets

Multi-segment filtering

Water washing, screen, filter cartridge or bag filter

Ventilation

Control wind speed and negative pressure in working area

construction maintenance

Equipment installation, filter material replacement and maintenance

Equipment and system configuration

01

Water-wash paint mist treatment

Capture paint mist with water curtain or wet washing method, suitable for high paint mist volume and continuous spraying operations.

02

Dry filter equipment

Use filter paper, bag type or HEPA filter material to intercept paint mist and fine dust, and maintain and replace it quickly.

03

Cartridge/bag filter dust collection

It is suitable for dry dust processes such as grinding, cutting, and polishing, and can be used with pulse backflush cleaning.

Typical project scope

Exhaust and supplementary air planning for spray paint booths

The connection between paint mist filtration and VOC post-processing

Grinding table, air collection hood and dust collection pipeline configuration

Integration of fans, air ducts, control panels and maintenance platforms

car parts furniture painting metal processing woodworking manufacturing surface treatment Machinery manufacturing
