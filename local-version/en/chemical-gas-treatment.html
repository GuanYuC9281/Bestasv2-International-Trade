Waste gas treatment project

Acid-base waste gas and volatile organic matter purification project

According to the type, concentration, temperature, humidity and emission standards of waste gas, we integrate scrubbers, activated carbon adsorption, air ducts, fans and control systems to help chemical, electroplating, PCB, semiconductor and painting processes stably meet emission standards.

design parameters

Pollutants:Acid mist, alkali mist, volatile organic compounds, odor

Material:PP, FRP, stainless steel and galvanized pipes

control:Pressure difference, liquid level, dosing and fan are linked

maintain:Consumable replacement and washing liquid management

Exhaust gas analysis

Confirm the nature and concentration of pollutants

Air volume calculation

Exhaust and pipeline impedance configuration according to process

Device integration

Washing, adsorption and exhaust systems are connected in series

Performance maintenance

Maintenance consumables and operating parameter recommendations

processing system module

01

PP/FRP scrubber

It is suitable for acid and alkali waste gas, water-soluble pollutants and acid mist treatment, and can be used with circulating water tanks and automatic dosing systems.

Corrosion resistant and easy maintenance

02

Activated carbon adsorption equipment

It is used for the terminal treatment of volatile organic compounds, odors and low-concentration organic waste gas. It can be designed in a drawer type or box type according to the needs.

Fast replacement of consumables

03

chemical washing system

The cleaning liquid and contact time are configured according to the acidity and alkalinity of the gas to improve neutralization and removal efficiency.

Can be used with pH control

04

Exhaust gas exhaust duct

Integrate the hood, air duct, fan and exhaust outlet to ensure that the process waste gas is effectively collected and sent to the treatment equipment.

Configure fans according to impedance
