Company Profile

Beida International Trading Co., Ltd. is committed to providing enterprises with high-efficiency, low-risk international procurement and supply chain solutions.

We have more than 20 years of practical experience in manufacturing, combine supply chain integration and one-stop engineering capabilities, and have an in-depth understanding of product characteristics, production processes and quality control keys. Assist customers to accurately match suitable suppliers, effectively reduce procurement risks and improve overall efficiency. By integrating supply chain resources in Taiwan, Vietnam and China, we provide one-stop services from procurement agency, quotation and proofing to production control and shipment delivery. Allowing enterprises to feel more secure, efficient and controllable during their cross-border procurement processes.
