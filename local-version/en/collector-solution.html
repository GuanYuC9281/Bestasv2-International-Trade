Product equipment

01

Pulse bag filter dust collector

Pulse compressed air is used for backflush cleaning, and continuous operation does not require downtime. It is suitable for all types of industrial dust, with high filtration efficiency and low maintenance costs.

Applicable:

cement factorysteel plantwood factory
02

Cylinder dust collector

The pleated cylindrical filter element has a large filtration area and small volume, and is suitable for precision processing, partial collection of chemical powders and metal dust.

Applicable:

CNC machiningmetal grindingChemical powder
03

cyclone separator

It uses centrifugal force to separate larger particle size dust without consuming filter material and requiring very little maintenance. It is often used as a pre-separator for bag filters to extend the life of the filter material.

Applicable:

sawdust collectionLarge particle size dustpreseparation
04

Electrostatic Precipitator (ESP)

It is suitable for fine dust and oil fume, with extremely low pressure loss. It is suitable for occasions where the use of filter materials is not allowed or low resistance is required to pass through. Regular cleaning of the dust collecting plate maintains efficiency.

Applicable:

kitchen fumesIndustrial oil miststeel industry
